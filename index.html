UNIQUE PACKAGING JARS AND BOTTLES

Unique Packaging Jars and Bottles is a Ugandan-based business that specializes in glass packaging for food and beverages. Additionally, we offer recyclable plastic containers for cosmetics and kraft pouch bags for powders and coffee.

Since its inception in 2021, Unique Packaging has been at the forefront of the packaging glass industry in Uganda. Our expertise lies in crafting innovative packaging solutions using glass, durable recyclable plastic, and kraft paper bags. At Unique Packaging, sustainability is at the core of our mission, driving us to prioritize environmentally-friendly packaging solutions.
